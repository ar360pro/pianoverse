Install PianoVerse as a Progressive Web App on any device. Works offline, no app store, no subscription required.

Windows & Mac

Open PianoVerse in Chrome or Edge, click the install icon in your browser bar, and launch like any desktop app.

Requires Chrome 73+ or Edge 79+
📱

iOS (iPhone & iPad)

Open in Safari → tap the Share button → select "Add to Home Screen". PianoVerse appears as a native app icon.

Requires iOS 11.3+ / Safari
🤖

Android

Open in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App". Full offline support included.

Requires Android 5+ / Chrome
